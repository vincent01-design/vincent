Started Reading Daily

On October 5, 2022, I decided to make reading a daily habit. I aimed to read any books whether it's fictional, non-fiction, personal development books, each day, but you can also say that some of what I'm reading it's not really a books but I consider it a book because it's also have a page or chapter more like of a books but the only different are the contents, though It's fictional but in some way you can learn a lot from it. This practice has helped me improve my vocabulary, expand my knowledge, and unwind after a busy day.

My favorite books/novels so far include:

  • "Atomic Habits" by James Clear
  • "Return of the Mount Hua Sect" by Biga
  • "Deep Work" by Cal Newport
  • "The Infinite Mage" by Kim chi Woo
  • "The Power of Now" by Eckhart Tolle
  • "The Beginning After the End" by Tae Ha Lee

Reading has become an essential part of my routine, and I plan to continue this journey for years to come.
