Learning Time Management

On September 2, 2023, I decided to get better at managing my time so I could balance school, personal projects, and free time. I started using a few simple methods that made a big difference in how I plan my day.

Here's what I did:

  • Worked in short sessions with breaks to stay focused and avoid burnout.
  • Used a daily planner to keep track of what I needed to do each day.
  • Set weekly goals so I always knew what I was working toward.

These changes have helped me get more done, stay organized, and still have time for myself.
